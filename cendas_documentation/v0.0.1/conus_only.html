cendas.conus_only

cendas.conus_only(data_frame: pandas.DataFrame(), tractcode_column: str)

Parameters/Arguments

  • data_frame
    • A pandas dataframe.
  • tractcode_column
    • The name of the column in the dataframe containing census tractcode/blockcode data as a string.

Description

Takes a pandas dataframe and the name of the dataframe column containing census tractcode/blockcode data as arguments. Parses tractcode data to filter out data from census tracts located outside the continental US (e.g. Hawaii, Alaska, Pureto Rico, etc.). Returns a pandas data frame with data only from tractcodes located within the continental US (CONUS).

Why This Method is Useful

There are always going to be stark statistical differences in many aspects between areas located within the continental United States and outside the continental United States. Many research endeavors, especially those involved with researching infratructure or funding related matters, differentiate between CONUS and OCONUS regions because the unique geographies of OCONUS regions warrant separate analyses. For example, the unique geogrphy of Alaska presents unique challenges to the development of broadband infratructure not present in the continental United States. As such, the inclusion of data from Alaska when researching broadband infrastructure with the rest of CONUS could lead to the inclusion of statistical outliers, which could skew results.
