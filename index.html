Justin Kim

My name is Justin Kim. I am currently an undergraduate statistics student and Data Science Institute Scholar at Columbia University expecting to graduate in May 2022. I am a research assistant at the Internet Real-Time Laboratory at Columbia University, where I am researching racial and regional discrepancies in federal broadband funding and adoption in the United States under the supervision of Prof. Henning Schulzrinne. I also have prior experience in iOS application development, software development, and process automation. In addition to all my technical experience, I speak English, German, Spanish, and Korean fluently.
