Short Bio

My name is Justin Kim. I am currently an undergraduate statistics student and Data Science Institute Scholar at Columbia University expecting to graduate in May 2022. I am a research assistant at the Computer Music Center at Columbia University, where I am conducting research in the field of program synthesis and computer music under the supervision of Prof. Mark Santolucito. I also have prior experience in iOS application development, software development, and process automation. I contribute occasionally to the Networkx developer community here:networkx-discuss@googlegroups.com, where I've documented some numerical errors associated with eigenvalue computation in the Networkx software package: https://github.com/justinkim668/networkx/blob/main/README.md. My interests lie in graph theory (as evidenced by my occasional contributions to the Networkx dev community), networks, machine learning, computer music, and acoustics. In addition to all my technical experience, I speak English, German, Spanish, and Korean fluently.
